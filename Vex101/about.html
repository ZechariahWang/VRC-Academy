Our Story

VRC Academy was founded in Calgary, Alberta, in 2022 by two 210Z team members, Zechariah Wang and Kevin Zhao. We are committed to making it easier for students to enter robotics by providing educational resources online and through the Vex Robotics Competition.

  • More than 3 years of experience in VRC
  • 7th in U.S (U.S Nationals) 8th in Canada (UO), 2nd in Alberta, top 1.5% in the world (UO)
  • 2x Tournament Champions, 2x Tournament Finalists, World Championship and U.S Open qualifiers, Worlds Sportsmanship Award

We're determined to share our knowledge and teach the next generation of innovators and creators.
